The Purpose

Goal: Introduce competitive programming to new students and interested students, as well as aid programming students in understanding complex data structures and algorithms. Educators can also utilize our primer to improve their teaching.

Motivation: Many students are interested in competitive programming, but don’t know where to start. There are also many more students who either are lacking in their understanding of data structure and algorithm fundamentals, or wish to deepen their knowledge of said material.
